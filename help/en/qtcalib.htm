QTopo - Calibration

With QTopoCalib you can download the calibration data from the DistoX, compute the calibration coefficients and write then to the DistoX. You can also toggle the DistoX between normal mode and calibration mode, and read the calibration coefficients from it. Finally you can save the calibration data to a file, that you can later re-open with QTopoCalib.
