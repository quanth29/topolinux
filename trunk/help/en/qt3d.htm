QTopo - 3D viewer

The 3D viewer displays the centeline and splay shots in 3D. The centerline shots are red, the splay shots are black. Cneterline shots are the thicker the closer they are to the viewpoint.

The point of view can be changed with the rotation buttons. The inclination goes from -90 to +90 degrees, while the azimuth goes al the way around the 360 degrees.
