QTopoShot - Shot edit


You open the shot edit dialog by clicking on a shot row in the table. This dialog (see image below) display six possible action. They are exclusive: you can pick only one of them, or none if you click the "Cancel" button.

  • Change the shot properties. You can add a comment to the shot. You can also change the "extend" flag and the additional flag.
  • Renumber the shots from this one onwards.
  • Merge the shot with the following one.
  • Split the shot in its components. This applies only to shots that are the average of more DistoX data.
  • Insert a new shot after (or before) this shot.
  • Delete the shot.
